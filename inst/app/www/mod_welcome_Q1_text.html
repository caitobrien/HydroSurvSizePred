This application can be used to explore factors affecting spring/summer Chinook salmon, Oncorhynchus tshawytscha, survival during downstream migration such as:

  • smolt length distributions,
  • predator risk from Northern Pikeminnow, Ptychocheilus oregonensis, and Pacific Hake, Merluccius productus,
  • and survival across years and seasons.

To interact with the app, select the SizePred Survival App tab in the side menu. To learn more about how this application can inform survival, please see Overview.
